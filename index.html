All Commands

m!help - see all bot commands
m!warn - Warn the said user.
m!ban - Ban the user.
m!kick - Kick the user from the server
m!mute - Mute the user and prevent them from speaking.
m!unmute - Unmute said user and allow them to speak again.
m!unban - Allow the user to join the server again.
And many more commands coming soon!

m!ping - Know the bot ping/response time.
m!autorole - Set a role that is given to all users on joining by default.
m!whois - Know the userinfo of said user.
And many more commands coming soon!

m!fart - Know the aftermath of your smellly fart.
m!eightball - Let the eightball decide your fate.
m!die - Know what happens after your death. People cry? Probably not.
m!kill - Know what happens when you kill someone you cold - hearted person!
And many more commands coming soon!
